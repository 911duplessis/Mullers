Their Story

Two sisters. One court. A dream that's real.

Mila and Lienka play for ORDMAK — a Canterbury basketball team competing at the Canterbury Recreation Centre and McAlpines Sports Centre in Christchurch. These two have shown up for every practice, every early start, every tight game. Basketball isn't just a sport for them. It's their thing.

Getting to the next level takes more than heart. Tournament fees, travel, gear, and training time stretch beyond what their family can cover alone. So they reached out, because they believed people would back them.

They were right to believe.

"Hello, it's Mila. Me and my sister need to raise $3,000."
— Mila, 2026

Every dollar goes directly to tournament entry, travel costs, and equipment. No admin fees. No overheads. Just two girls, a ball, and a genuine shot at something bigger.

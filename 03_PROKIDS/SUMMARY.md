# ProKids / Proteus — Summary

A children's emotional readiness and movement programme for ages 2–8 (possibly 3–16 in the full vision), based in Canterbury, New Zealand. Conceived and developed through deep AI-assisted strategy research between Stiaan and Elanza during June 2026.

## Working names
- **"ProKids"** — Stiaan's working name
- **"Proteus"** / **"Proteus Kids Sports"** — Elanza's preferred direction (May 14–15)
- **"ProStars"** — Elanza floated June 3 ("Dalk Prostars?")
- **"Inner Readiness"** — Stiaan's strategic category-naming frame (not a brand name, but a positioning claim — see below)

## The strategic concept (Stiaan's most important contribution)
> "The category is Inner Readiness. Not a programme name. Not a brand tagline. A new field — the same way Montessori named a field, Waldorf named a field, and SEL named a field. The category claim is: there is a form of readiness that is prior to academic skill, prior to physical fitness, and prior to any specialist learning. It lives in the nervous system, the social brain, and the body. It has been undernamed, underdeveloped, and underserved. That's the white space." *(chat line ~11133)*

Key positioning insight: **movement is one tool in the delivery, not the product itself.** The product is "a parent who can see their child becoming more okay, week by week." This distinguishes ProKids from sport/movement classes and from child psychology — it sits in the pricing and positioning gap between them.

## Canterbury market evidence (from Stiaan's research, ~line 11108)
- Child psychology waitlist crisis: 53% of NZ parents face one
- Post-pandemic social cognition deficit confirmed in 2026 research
- Dominant parenting trend toward analog, screen-free, structured play
- "None of those point to sport. All three point to the same gap."

## Pricing logic
- Sport class in Canterbury: $12–18/session
- Child psychology: $150–250/session
- Stiaan's positioning target: **$22–25/session** — "in the gap between them, which justifies $22–25/session without requiring parents to understand the pedagogy." *(line ~11114)*

## Income scenarios built (Year 1)
- **Option A:** Great local business at $65–85k, working 15 hours/week
- **Option B:** Regional brand + certification play at $120–180k by Year 3
- **Option C:** National movement, $300k+ by Year 5 — "could be the next Montessori"
- All three start identically — Elanza doesn't need to choose her ambition level yet

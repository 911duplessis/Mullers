Who are they?

Two sisters chasing the
same dream

Mila and Lienka are Christchurch girls who live and breathe sport — basketball, competitive events, and everything in between. They're at the age where opportunity knocks once, and where showing up matters more than anything.

Our mum, Elanza, works hard to give us every opportunity she can. We want to do something she can be proud of — and we need a little help from our community to get there.

We're not asking for a handout. We're asking for a hand. Every dollar you give goes directly toward our sports journey — equipment, registrations, and the things that make the difference between watching from the sideline and stepping onto the court.

We raised $3,000 as our target because that's exactly what it costs to get us where we need to be. Not a cent more. And we promise to make every one of those dollars count.

Our uncle Stiaan built our cousin Kira-Lee a page just like this one for her hockey tour — and the Christchurch community showed up. We're asking you to show up for us too.

🏀 Mila

Competitive basketball player, waterpolo veteran, and all-round athlete. Mila has represented her school across multiple sports and isn't slowing down.

⭐ Lienka

Lienka brings the energy and the heart. Together with Mila, they've grown up on the sidelines of every sport imaginable — now it's their turn to compete.

📍 Christchurch, New Zealand

Born and raised in Christchurch, this family knows what community means. If you've ever been helped by someone in this city, now's your chance to pay it forward.

Who's Behind It

Elanza Muller. Independent. Canterbury-based.

Elanza Muller is a South African expat based in Christchurch, New Zealand. She built EM Services to serve the SA community across Canterbury — people who need real support navigating the police clearance process from the other side of the world.

With a teacher's degree and years of experience in administrative and educational contexts, Elanza brings precision, care, and genuine responsiveness to every client. She manages the entire SAPS submission process on your behalf — something no automated online portal can offer.

She also has two daughters who play basketball for ORDMAK and is developing a children's programme (ProKids) set to launch in Canterbury schools. EM Services is a growing ecosystem, not just a single service.
